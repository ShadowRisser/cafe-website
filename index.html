Our Story

A Legacy of Excellence

15+

Years of Craft

Born from Passion, Perfected by Time

Founded in 2009, Maison Café was born from a simple belief: that coffee is more than a beverage — it's an art form. Our journey began in a small Parisian-inspired corner, where the aroma of freshly roasted beans met the warmth of genuine hospitality.

Today, we continue that tradition with single-origin beans sourced directly from family farms in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Guatemala. Each cup is a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality, sustainability, and the craft of exceptional coffee.
